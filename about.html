About Kelv

I am a UX/UI Designer based in Nairobi, Kenya.


From my experience and from what I think, the ultimate goal of design in product developement is to create a good or service with excellent functional utility to achieve the desired product quality and to ensure availability of the product according to the needs of the users within a short time.

2014
This is where my passion for computing started.
While in high school, I've always wanted to know how things, to be precise, electronics worked. This led me into pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.
Design popped out as the path to take when I joined KamiLimu and I got to understand user experience, product design and got mentors who helped shaped my passion pretty well.
January 2020
Never stop learning
Over the years I have always sought to add into my knowledge of what I know. I believe there's always something new to be learnt everyday. So why not be open-minded?
After completing my undergrad, I enrolled into Interaction Design Foundation to advance my UX skills.
Today
3 yrs experience and counting
I've since transitioned from Design Engineer Intern at Adrian Kenya to Website / Visual Designer at Dotsh to UX/UI Designer at e.Kraal Innovation Hub to Senior UI/UX Engineer at TheBhub and still aspiring to dive into Product Design eventually.
